ABOUT US

We pivot your business to the next level

Blucon Consulting Limited is a Pan-African advisory and management consulting firm that provides solutions in the fields of Project Management, Strategy and Business planning, Feasibility Studies, Research, Tourism and Hospitality, Marketing and Sustainable Development opportunities for organizations.

Blucon Consulting prides itself in understanding the rigors of entrepreneurship. We have built invaluable expertise in maneuvering business undertakings from idea stages to actualization. In the Coast region particularly, we have pivoted to become the premier referral partner for entrepreneurial penetration into existing and emerging industries.

With numerous opportunities in the Blue Economy, Special Economic Zones, Shipping and Logistics, Agri-business and Manufacturing, Blucon Consulting offers to walk with you from process conceptualization, to the technicalities of licensing, registration, capital planning and eventual business set-up.We thrive in collaborative partnerships that offer professional foresighted execution across all stages of business.

We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to Consultancy. Our aim is to transform businesses from the inside, through offering cutting edge client specific solutions. We draw insight from client data, building flexible, future-oriented and resilient businesses.
